## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I add a new friary?
**A:** Contact your Provincial Administrator. Only admins can add new communities.

### Q: Why don't I see any documents?
**A:** Documents need to be tagged with a friary ID when uploaded. Check with your admin.

### Q: Can I edit friary information?
**A:** Only if you're the Guardian of that friary or a Provincial Admin.

### Q: How is budget calculated?
**A:** Monthly budget is set by Provincial Treasurer. Expenses are tracked from receipts.

### Q: What if expenses exceed budget?
**A:** The system shows a warning. Contact Provincial Treasurer for budget adjustment.

### Q: Can I see other friaries' finances?
**A:** Depends on your role. Staff see limited info, Admins see everything.
